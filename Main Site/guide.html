Installation

Follow The Steps To Download The Hub:

  1. Go to the main site and click the Download button.
  2. Double click on the downloaded file
  3. Open the index.html file. It should open in Chrome
  4. You are good to play any games in the hub!

Safety Guide

A guide on the best methods to play games without the teachers noticing.

  1. After downloading, if you don't need it, disconnect from WiFi.
  2. Have a second Chrome window open to Alt+Tab To Quickly in case a teacher walks by.
